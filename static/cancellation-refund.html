Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2025

1. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your BhashAI subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting our support team.

  • Self-Service Cancellation: Log into your dashboard and navigate to Account Settings > Billing
  • Email Cancellation: Send a cancellation request to support@bhashai.com
  • Phone Cancellation: Call our support team at 18002330000
  • Effective Date: Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer refunds under the following circumstances:

  • 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee: Full refund for new subscriptions within 7 days
  • Service Outages: Pro-rated refunds for extended service interruptions
  • Billing Errors: Full refund for incorrect charges or duplicate payments
  • Unused Credits: Refund of unused prepaid credits upon account closure

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

  • Usage-based charges for completed voice calls and API requests
  • Phone number rental fees for numbers already provisioned
  • Third-party service fees (Twilio, ElevenLabs, etc.)
  • Custom development or integration services
  • Subscriptions cancelled after the 7-day guarantee period

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team with your refund request
  2. Provide your account details and reason for the refund
  3. Our team will review your request within 2-3 business days
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  5. Refunds will be credited to your original payment method

5. Partial Refunds

In certain cases, we may offer partial refunds:

  • Pro-rated Refunds: For mid-cycle cancellations due to service issues
  • Downgrade Refunds: Credit difference when downgrading plans mid-cycle
  • Service Credits: Account credits for service disruptions or issues

6. Enterprise and Custom Plans

Enterprise and custom plan cancellations and refunds are subject to the terms outlined in your specific agreement. Please contact your account manager or our enterprise support team for assistance.

7. Free Trial Cancellation

Free trials can be cancelled at any time without charge. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, you'll be automatically charged for the selected plan.

  • Cancel anytime during the trial period
  • No charges if cancelled before trial expiration
  • Automatic conversion to paid plan if not cancelled
  • Email reminders sent before trial expiration

8. Account Suspension vs. Cancellation

Understanding the difference between account suspension and cancellation:

  • Suspension: Temporary pause due to payment issues or policy violations
  • Cancellation: Permanent termination of services and account closure
  • Reactivation: Suspended accounts can be reactivated by resolving issues
  • Data Retention: Account data retained for 30 days after cancellation

9. Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with our refund decision, you may:

  • Request escalation to our management team
  • Provide additional documentation supporting your case
  • Contact your payment provider for chargeback assistance
  • Seek resolution through applicable consumer protection agencies

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Cancellation and Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

11. Contact Information

For cancellation requests or refund inquiries, please contact us:

Email: support@bhashai.com

Phone: 18002330000

Support Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM IST

Response Time: Within 24 hours for refund requests
