Shipping and Delivery Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2025

1. Service Nature

BhashAI provides digital AI voice services that are delivered electronically. As a cloud-based software service, we do not ship physical products. All services are delivered instantly upon account activation and payment confirmation.

2. Digital Service Delivery

Our services are delivered through the following methods:

  • Instant Access: Dashboard and API access provided immediately after payment
  • Email Notifications: Account credentials and setup instructions sent via email
  • API Endpoints: Integration details provided through our developer portal
  • Documentation: Complete guides and tutorials available online
  • Support Resources: Help center and knowledge base accessible 24/7

3. Account Activation Timeline

Service activation times vary based on your plan and requirements:

  • Free Trial: Instant activation upon email verification
  • Standard Plans: Immediate access after payment confirmation
  • Enterprise Plans: 1-2 business days for custom setup and configuration
  • Custom Integrations: 3-5 business days depending on complexity
  • Phone Number Provisioning: 15 minutes to 24 hours based on provider

4. Phone Number Delivery

When you purchase phone numbers through our platform:

  • Instant Numbers: Most numbers are available immediately
  • Verification Required: Some numbers may require identity verification
  • Geographic Restrictions: Certain regions may have longer provisioning times
  • Carrier Approval: Business numbers may need carrier approval (1-3 days)
  • Configuration: Numbers are automatically configured for your account

5. API and Integration Delivery

For developers and technical integrations:

  • API Keys: Generated instantly upon account creation
  • Webhook URLs: Provided immediately for real-time integrations
  • SDK Downloads: Available through our developer portal
  • Code Examples: Comprehensive samples in multiple programming languages
  • Testing Environment: Sandbox access for development and testing

6. Service Availability

Our services are available globally with the following considerations:

  • Global Access: Platform accessible from anywhere with internet connection
  • Regional Features: Some features may vary by geographic location
  • Language Support: Hindi, English, and Hinglish available worldwide
  • Compliance: Services comply with local telecommunications regulations
  • Data Centers: Multiple regions for optimal performance

7. Delivery Confirmation

You will receive confirmation of service delivery through:

  • Welcome Email: Sent immediately after successful payment
  • Dashboard Access: Login credentials and account details
  • Setup Guide: Step-by-step instructions for getting started
  • Support Contact: Direct access to our technical support team
  • Service Status: Real-time status updates in your dashboard

8. Delivery Issues and Support

If you experience any issues with service delivery:

  • Check Email: Verify spam/junk folders for delivery notifications
  • Account Status: Log into your dashboard to check activation status
  • Contact Support: Reach out to our team for immediate assistance
  • Live Chat: Available during business hours for real-time help
  • Priority Support: Enterprise customers receive priority assistance

9. Service Level Agreements (SLA)

We maintain high service availability standards:

  • Uptime Guarantee: 99.9% service availability
  • Response Time: API responses within 200ms average
  • Support Response: Within 4 hours for critical issues
  • Maintenance Windows: Scheduled during low-usage periods
  • Status Page: Real-time service status at status.bhashai.com

10. Third-Party Dependencies

Some services depend on third-party providers:

  • Telecom Providers: Phone number provisioning may be delayed by carriers
  • AI Services: Voice synthesis depends on upstream AI providers
  • Payment Processing: Account activation depends on payment confirmation
  • Regulatory Approval: Some features require compliance verification
